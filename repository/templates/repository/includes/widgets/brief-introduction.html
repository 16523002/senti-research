SentiResearch101

SentiResearch is web-based application system that allows the user researcher and other role needed in doing qualitative research, to store, manage, and schedule research project and generate its sentiment based on the narrative of in-depth interview.

The primary motivation behind building this application is to provide a single automated platform, which serves as a complete end-to-end system for sentiment analysis of customer feedbacks in interview session along with the data visualization.

User Interview101

What is User Interview?

In-depth interview is a qualitative research technique that involves conducting intensive individual interviews with a small number of respondents to explore their perspectives on a particular idea, program, or situation.

Why Do User Interviews?

Interviews give insights into what users think about a site, an application, a product, or a process. They can point out what site content is memorable, what people feel is important on the site, and what ideas for improvement they may have.
